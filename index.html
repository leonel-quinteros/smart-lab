Research Focus

The SM-Art Laboratory conducts interdisciplinary research at the Department of Civil Engineering, Universidad de Chile. Our work spans:

  • Structural Materials: Analysis and development of advanced materials for construction and infrastructure.
  • Multiscale Mechanics: Bridging scales from atomistic to macroscopic behavior of materials.
  • Computational Methods: Developing and applying numerical techniques for complex engineering problems.

About the Lab

Based at the Civil Engineering Department of Universidad de Chile, SM-Art Lab is committed to conducting world-class research, training the next generation of engineers and scientists, and contributing to the advancement of civil and materials engineering.
