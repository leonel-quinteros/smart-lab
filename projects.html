Active research projects, collaborative initiatives, software tools, and datasets from SM-Art Lab.

CNT-Based Composites for Smart Infrastructure

Goal: Develop and characterize carbon nanotube composites with enhanced conductivity and self-sensing capabilities for structural health monitoring and damage detection.

Team: Leonel Quinteros, Tomás Rojas (manufacturing and standardization)

Focus Areas: Conductive cementitious samples, electrical-mechanical coupling, multifunctional materials

Related Publications

Corrosion-Driven Degradation & Integrity Prediction

Goal: Predict structural integrity in corroded cables and infrastructure using phase-field methods and advanced computational techniques.

Team: Leonel Quinteros, Ignacio Araya (phase-field modeling)

Focus Areas: Phase-field fracture mechanics, cable corrosion, degradation modeling, predictive maintenance

Related Publications

Phononic Band Gap Optimization

Goal: Design and optimize phononic structures for vibration isolation and dynamic damping in civil engineering applications.

Team: Leonel Quinteros

Focus Areas: Cellular structures, sandwich panels, topology optimization, band gap scalability
